Struggling with disorganized contact lists?

Contact management is the foundation of any outreach campaign. Stop wasting time on chaotic spreadsheets, duplicate numbers, and outdated entries. WA Campaign Sender introduces a streamlined, built-in system that saves you time and boosts accuracy.

The Hidden Costs of Manual Contact Management

Manual copy-paste tasks slow you down.

Errors creep in, versions get messy, and your valuable time is spent fixing issues instead of running effective campaigns.

The WA Campaign Sender Solution

With our browser extension, you get two powerful tools:

CSV Importer and Contact Extractor — both work directly in your browser to centralize and simplify contact list management.

1. Effortless CSV Importing

Have a contact list from your CRM or Excel?

Just export it as a CSV file and upload it with WA Campaign Sender. Our importer supports mapping custom fields like {{Name}} or {{OrderID}}, so your messages stay personalized.

2. One-Click Contact Extractor

Build your campaign list without leaving WhatsApp Web.

Our extractor allows you to:

  • Extract from Any Group: Instantly capture all members.
  • Multiple Groups: Select several and extract at once.
  • Chat List: Get contacts from recent conversations.
  • Saved Contacts: Export your entire WhatsApp phonebook.

No more jumping between tabs or copy-pasting numbers.
Note: Always ensure you have permission before contacting anyone.

Simple Steps to Build Lists

  • Existing Lists: Export as CSV, upload, map fields, and go.
  • From WhatsApp: Use the extractor to create clean, instant lists for campaigns.

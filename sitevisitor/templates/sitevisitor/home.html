How do We Compare

Understand the key differences between using our extension and the official WhatsApp Business API.

WA Campaign Sender

KEY ADVANTAGES:

  • Quick & Easy Setup: Get started in minutes, no technical skills needed.
  • No Per-Message Fees: Send campaigns without worrying about costs per message.
  • Send Any Message Content: Full freedom to craft personalized messages without template restrictions.
  • Use Your Existing Number: Works with your personal or standard WhatsApp account.
  • No Approval Delays: Start sending immediately without waiting for API approvals.
  • Limited Advanced Automation: Best for direct outreach, not complex chatbot flows.

WhatsApp Business API

KEY CONSIDERATIONS:

  • Complex Implementation: Requires technical expertise and development resources.
  • Per-Message & Setup Costs: Involves variable costs based on usage and provider.
  • Strict Message Templates: Outbound messages often restricted to pre-approved templates.
  • Requires Dedicated Business Number: Cannot typically use a personal WhatsApp number.
  • Lengthy Approval Process: Business verification and API access can take time.
  • Powerful Automation & Chatbots: Ideal for complex, large-scale automated interactions.

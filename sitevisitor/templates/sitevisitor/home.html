How do We Compare

Understand the key differences between using our extension and WhatsApp API-based services.

WA Campaign Sender

KEY ADVANTAGES:

  • Quick & Easy Setup: Get started in minutes, no technical skills needed.
  • No Per-Message Fees: Send campaigns without worrying about costs per message.
  • Send Any Message Content: Full freedom to craft personalized messages without template restrictions.

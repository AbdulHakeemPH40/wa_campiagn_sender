About WA Campaign Sender

Your trusted WhatsApp marketing solution with powerful API integration

Who We Are

WA Campaign Sender is a comprehensive WhatsApp marketing platform that combines powerful API integration with an intuitive dashboard. We help businesses engage with their customers through personalized messaging, AI-powered content generation, and advanced campaign management.

Our platform connects seamlessly with WhatsApp through secure API integration, enabling you to send personalized bulk messages, manage contacts, track campaign performance in real-time, and maintain compliance with automated opt-out handling.

Platform Features

WhatsApp API Integration

Secure connection to WhatsApp with QR code session management and real-time status updates.

AI-Powered Drafts

Generate professional marketing messages with OpenAI integration and content moderation.

Contact Management

Import contacts via Excel/CSV, organize with custom lists, and manage personalization fields.

Campaign Analytics

Real-time tracking of sent, delivered, read, and failed messages with detailed reports.

Opt-Out Management

Automatic STOP keyword detection and opt-out list management for compliance.

Media Attachments

Send images, PDFs, and documents with your campaigns via Cloudinary integration.

Session Protection

Built-in rate limiting and smart delays to protect your WhatsApp account from bans.

Draft Templates

Save and reuse message templates with personalization variables like {first_name}.

To empower businesses of all sizes with effective WhatsApp marketing tools that are both powerful and user-friendly. We believe in making WhatsApp marketing accessible to everyone while maintaining the highest standards of safety, compliance, and deliverability.

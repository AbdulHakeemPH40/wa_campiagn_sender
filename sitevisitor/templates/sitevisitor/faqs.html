Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

WA Campaign Sender is a browser extension for Chrome and Edge that enhances your WhatsApp Web experience. It allows you to send personalized bulk messages, manage contact lists, and track campaign progress directly within your browser, eliminating the need for complex APIs or external tools.

From the extension's main menu, click on "Message Sender." You can then choose your target audience (from saved lists, group members, or by uploading a CSV), compose your message with personalization variables, attach media if needed, and start the campaign. You can monitor the progress in real-time.

The Contact Extractor is a powerful feature that allows you to build contact lists directly from WhatsApp. You can extract phone numbers from groups you are a member of, from your existing chat list, or even from saved contact cards. This is a quick way to build targeted lists for your campaigns.

We prioritize your account's safety. Our extension uses intelligent, randomized delays between messages to simulate human behavior and reduce the risk of being flagged by WhatsApp. However, we always recommend following best practices, such as sending messages only to users who expect to hear from you and avoiding spammy content.

The Free Plan is a great way to get started. It allows you to connect one WhatsApp account and send unlimited messages. However, it does not include premium features like 12/7 WhatsApp support. For more advanced features and support, please consider upgrading to one of our paid plans.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our WhatsApp API platform? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

WA Campaign Sender is a cloud-based WhatsApp API platform that allows you to send bulk WhatsApp campaigns from our web dashboard. Simply connect your WhatsApp by scanning a QR code, upload your contact list, compose your message with personalization, and send campaigns - all from any device (mobile, tablet, or laptop). No extensions or software installation required!

Sending a campaign is easy:

  1. Connect WhatsApp: Scan the QR code from our dashboard to link your WhatsApp
  2. Upload Contacts: Import your contact list via CSV or Excel file
  3. Compose Message: Write your message with personalization variables like {first_name}
  4. Add Attachments: Optionally attach images, videos, PDFs, or audio
  5. Send Campaign: Click send and monitor progress in real-time

Campaigns run in the background on our servers - you can close your browser and check back anytime!

You can upload contacts via CSV or Excel (XLSX) files. Your file should include phone numbers (with country code) and can include additional fields like name, email, etc. for personalization. Our system automatically detects columns and lets you map them to variables you can use in your messages like {first_name}, {company}, etc.

We prioritize your account's safety with built-in protection features:

  • Smart Delays: Randomized delays between messages to simulate human behavior
  • Batch Sending: Send in batches with cooldown periods
  • Account Protection Mode: Extra safety settings for new accounts

⚠️ Important: We are not responsible for any WhatsApp account bans. Please use the platform respectfully, follow WhatsApp's terms of service, and avoid sending spam or unsolicited messages.

The number of messages depends on your WhatsApp account's age and warmup status:

  • New accounts (0-7 days): Start with 50-100 messages
  • Warming up (1-4 weeks): 100-300 messages
  • Established (1+ month): 300-500 messages

Always start low and gradually increase to keep your account safe.

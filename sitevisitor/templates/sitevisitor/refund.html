REFUND POLICY

Effective Date: December 8, 2025

OUR COMMITMENT

At WA Campaign Sender, we believe in building trust through fair and transparent policies. We understand that sometimes things don't work out as expected, and we're committed to treating every customer with respect and understanding.

Our Promise: For genuine cases, we will process your refund — no unnecessary questions, no hassle.

REFUND ELIGIBILITY

We consider refund requests on a case-by-case basis. The following situations are generally eligible for a refund:

  • • Technical Issues — Service not functioning as described and our team is unable to resolve the issue
  • • Accidental Purchase — Duplicate payment or unintended subscription (reported within 48 hours)
  • • Service Unavailability — Extended downtime preventing you from using the platform
  • • Billing Errors — Incorrect charges or payment processing issues

REFUND WINDOW

7-Day Window: Refund requests should be submitted within 7 days of purchase for fastest processing.

Requests made after 7 days will still be reviewed, but approval depends on the specific circumstances and usage of the service.

HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND

To request a refund, please follow these simple steps:

  1. 1 Email us at hi@wacampaignsender.com with subject line "Refund Request"
  2. 2 Include your registered email address and date of purchase
  3. 3 Briefly describe the reason for your request
  4. 4 Our team will review and respond within 24-48 hours

PROCESSING TIME

  • • Review Time — 24-48 hours from request submission
  • • Refund Processing — 5-7 business days after approval
  • • Payment Method — Refunds are issued to the original payment method (PayPal or Razorpay)

NON-REFUNDABLE SITUATIONS

While we strive to be fair, the following situations are generally not eligible for refunds:

  • • WhatsApp account restrictions or bans (not within our control)
  • • Change of mind after extensive use of the service
  • • Failure to read service features before purchase
  • • Violation of our Terms of Service leading to account suspension

OUR PROMISE TO YOU

Fair Treatment — Every request is reviewed individually with understanding

Quick Response — We respond to all refund requests within 24-48 hours

No Hidden Fees — Approved refunds are processed in full, no deductions

Genuine Support — We'd rather solve your problem than lose you as a customer

BEFORE REQUESTING A REFUND

If you're experiencing issues with our service, please reach out to our support team first. Many problems can be resolved quickly:

  • • Technical issues can often be fixed within hours
  • • Our team can guide you through features you may have missed
  • • We may be able to offer alternative solutions that work better for you

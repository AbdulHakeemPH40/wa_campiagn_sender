Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to WA Campaign Sender ("WA Campaign Sender", "we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, store, and safeguard your data when you access or use our browser extension, website, and related services (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to be bound by it.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimal information necessary for authentication and service provision:

  • Authentication Data — We collect only your name, email address, and mobile number for account authentication purposes.
  • No Device Tracking — We do not collect or track your device information, location data, or browsing habits.
  • No Campaign Data Storage — Your campaign details and WhatsApp contacts are stored locally in your browser only. This data is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • Minimal Cookies — We use only essential cookies required for basic website functionality and authentication.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your authentication data (name, email, mobile number) is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption. All other data, including your campaign details and contacts, remains exclusively on your device and is never accessed by us.

4. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA or UK, we process your limited Personal Data when:

  • It is necessary to perform our service contract with you;
  • You have given explicit consent during registration (which you may withdraw at any time);
  • We must comply with a legal obligation.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use your authentication information (name, email, mobile number) only to:

  • Authenticate your account and verify your identity;
  • Provide access to the Service you've purchased;
  • Communicate essential service updates and account notifications;
  • Process payments and maintain billing records;
  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries;
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations.

6. Data Retention

We keep your authentication data (name, email, mobile number) only as long as your account remains active. Upon account deletion, we permanently remove all your personal information from our systems within 30 days.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your limited authentication data. Your campaign data and contacts remain exclusively on your device and are never exposed to security risks on our end.

8. Payment Processing

We use trusted third-party payment processors to handle all financial transactions. We do not store your payment information on our servers. Payment processors may collect additional information as required for transaction processing, subject to their own privacy policies.

9. No Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes. Your authentication data is used solely for providing our service to you.

10. Local Storage of Campaign Data

All your campaign data, contact lists, message templates, and campaign settings are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. This approach maximizes your privacy while allowing you to use our service effectively.

11. Links to Other Sites

The Service may link to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies before providing data.

10. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us.

13. Your Data Protection Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the limited personal data we hold about you;
  • Correct any inaccurate information in your account;
  • Delete your account and all associated personal data;
  • Object to the processing of your personal data;
  • Export your authentication data in a portable format.

To exercise these rights, simply email us at the address below. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will appear here with a new “Last updated” date. Continued use of the Service after changes indicates acceptance.

PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: December 8, 2025

1. INTRODUCTION

WA Campaign Sender ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates a fully cloud-based WhatsApp marketing platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

By accessing or using our Service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue using the Service immediately.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following information when you create an account:

  • Name & Email Address — For authentication and communication
  • Phone Number — For account verification and WhatsApp session linking
  • Password — Securely hashed using bcrypt

2.2 Service Data

To operate our messaging system, we securely process:

  • Contact Lists — Phone numbers uploaded by you
  • Message Content — Templates, captions, and text for campaigns
  • Campaign Logs — Delivery reports and message history
  • Media Files — Temporarily stored images, videos, and documents
  • WhatsApp Session Credentials — Encrypted to maintain your connection

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

For security and fraud prevention, we collect login timestamps, device identifiers, and session details.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information to:

  • Operate and improve our cloud-based service
  • Process and deliver your WhatsApp campaigns via our API system
  • Verify your identity and secure your account
  • Provide customer support and service notifications
  • Manage billing, subscriptions, and transactions
  • Enforce legal and compliance requirements

Users are solely responsible for ensuring that all recipients have provided consent to receive WhatsApp messages, in accordance with applicable laws and WhatsApp policies.

4. DATA SECURITY

We employ strong security measures to protect your data:

  • TLS/SSL Encryption during data transmission
  • AES-256 and Fernet Encryption for stored sensitive data
  • Isolated Cloud Infrastructure hosted on secure servers
  • Strict Access Controls for internal security
  • bcrypt Password Hashing for account protection

5. DATA RETENTION

  • Account Information — Stored while your account is active
  • Campaign Logs — Retained for 12 months
  • Media Files — Deleted within 30 days
  • Account Deletion Requests — Processed within 30 days

6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use trusted third-party providers to operate our platform:

  • Payment Processors — PayPal and Razorpay
  • Cloud Storage Providers — For secure media hosting
  • Messaging Infrastructure Provider — A third-party WhatsApp messaging API provider processes phone numbers, message text, and media solely for campaign delivery.

All providers operate under their own privacy policies and comply with industry security standards.

7. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do not sell or trade your personal data. We may disclose information only when necessary:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our platform and users
  • When you explicitly authorize it
  • During business transfers (with notice)

8. YOUR RIGHTS

You may request the following at any time:

  • Access to your data
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your data
  • Data portability
  • Restriction or objection to processing
  • Withdrawal of consent

To exercise your rights, contact us at hi@wacampaignsender.com.

9. COOKIES

We use only essential cookies required for login, security, and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years old. If we learn that a minor has submitted data, we will remove it promptly.

11. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

Data may be processed outside your home country. We implement safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses when required.

12. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be announced on our website or via email.

13. CONTACT US

For questions related to this Privacy Policy, contact us:

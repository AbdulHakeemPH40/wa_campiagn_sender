WhatsApp Policy Guidelines

WhatsApp Broadcast Feature Updates

WhatsApp has updated its broadcast functionality for business accounts. Understanding these changes is important for maintaining compliance with WhatsApp's terms of service.

Important Information:
  • • Do not use old-style broadcast groups or 256-member groups to send bulk messages as this may violate WhatsApp's terms of service
  • • Using broadcast lists for internal team communication is not recommended
  • • Ensure your WhatsApp application is updated to the latest version
  • • Using outdated broadcast features may affect your account status
WhatsApp Messaging Options:
Standard Account Features:
  • • Limited to 256 contacts per broadcast
  • • Media restrictions apply to broadcasts
  • • Each broadcast must be created individually
  • • No permanent broadcast groups
Business API Benefits:
  • • Per-message pricing structure
  • • Regional pricing varies by country
  • • Pre-approved templates required
  • • Higher volume messaging capabilities

WhatsApp Business Best Practices

Your WhatsApp business number is a valuable communication asset. Following these best practices helps maintain account health and optimize message delivery.

Activities to Avoid:

  • • Excessive message volume (over 1,000 messages/day)
  • • Repetitive messaging patterns
  • • Identical messages to multiple contacts
  • • Using deprecated messaging features
  • • Using the same number on multiple devices simultaneously

Recommended Practices:

  • • Focus on quality over quantity in your contact list
  • • Schedule campaigns weekly rather than daily
  • • Distribute messaging across multiple numbers
  • • Personalize messages with recipient information
  • • Monitor message delivery rates regularly

Use WhatsApp Business responsibly and in accordance with the platform's terms of service.

Account Health Guidelines

Recommended Practices

  • Use 3+ month old accounts
  • Follow warm-up schedule
  • Sync contacts via Google
  • Update WhatsApp regularly
  • Use one device per number

Practices to Avoid

  • Use new numbers for bulk messaging
  • Send from old broadcast lists
  • Forward messages during campaigns
  • Use same number on multiple devices
  • Send 1000+ messages on day 1

Common WhatsApp Marketing Mistakes

Message Content Mistakes

  • Sending generic, non-personalized messages
  • Using excessive emojis or ALL CAPS text
  • Including too many links or attachments
  • Writing overly long messages that require scrolling

Campaign Strategy Mistakes

  • Messaging at inappropriate times (late night/early morning)
  • Sending too many messages in a short timeframe
  • Not segmenting your audience properly
  • Failing to track and analyze campaign results

Compliance Mistakes to Avoid

  • • Messaging users without explicit opt-in consent
  • • Not providing a clear opt-out option in your messages
  • • Sharing customer data without proper authorization
  • • Ignoring local data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
  • • Not maintaining proper records of user consent
